Darth Vader

The chosen one in a far away galaxy a very long time ago

Anakin Skywalker, former Jedi turned Darth Vader, brandishes the red lightsaber as symbolism of the sith.

Here's a timeline of Darth Vader's life:

  • 41 BBY - Born to Shmi Skywalker on Tatooine
  • 48 BBY - Rebuilt protocol droid C-3PO using scavenged parts
  • 50 BBY - Begins jedi training and earns the Padawan rank
  • 53 BBY - Becomes apprentice to Jedi Obi-Wan Kenobi
  • 56 BBY - Earns rank of Jedi Knight
  • 59 BBY - Meets Padme Amidala
  • 61 BBY - Participates in Clone Wars battle with Obi-Wan Kenobi, before losing hand in aclightsaber battle with Count Dooku
  • 62 BBY - Marries Padme in secret wedding ceremony
  • 64 BBY - Joins Emperor Palpatine as Sith Lord Vader in hopes to save Padme
  • 64 BBY - Battles Obi-Wan Kenobi and becomes mortally wounded
  • 64 BBY - Saved by Emperor Palpatine, Darth Vader rises
  • 80 BBY - Kills Obi-Wan Kenobi in lightsaber rematch
  • 85 BBY - Battles son, Luke Skywalker, in first lightsaber duel cutting off Luke's hand
  • 90 BBY - Battles Luke and is ultimately defeated after which he comes to the light
  • 90 BBY - Kills Emperor Palpatine
  • 91 BBY - Dies in Luke's presence after apologizing for all his sithful deeds
